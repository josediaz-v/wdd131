Site Plan for Sparkling Clean Website

1. Site Name

Site Name: Sparkling Clean

Reason for Selection: The name "Sparkling Clean" was chosen to reflect the essence of the business — a cleaning service that promises immaculate results. It is easy to remember, relevant to the services offered, and directly conveys the quality and cleanliness the brand stands for.

Optional Domain Availability: sparklingcleanservices.com (Available at the time of checking)

2. Site Purpose

The website serves as a digital hub for Sparkling Clean, a company offering various cleaning services. Its primary functions are:

  • Service Overview: The site introduces the cleaning services provided — including residential, office, and deep cleaning — with details about what each service includes and pricing.
  • Booking Form: A form that allows customers to easily book cleaning services by selecting a preferred date, time, and service type.
  • Testimonials: A section where customers can read positive feedback from others to gain trust in the service.
  • Contact Form: A contact form for inquiries, allowing potential clients to reach out for quotes or additional information.

The website aims to provide an accessible platform where visitors can easily learn about the services, book a cleaning, and contact the business.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1:

Question: What types of cleaning services do you offer, and how much do they cost?

Content: The site will have detailed descriptions of each cleaning service, including residential, office, and deep cleaning, along with their respective pricing starting points.

Scenario 2:

Question: How can I book a cleaning service?

Content: Users will be able to select their preferred service, date, and time through a simple booking form. The site will show the process of booking and how long it takes to schedule.

4. Color Schema

The color scheme is designed to promote a fresh and professional look, while ensuring readability and contrast.

Primary Color: #8d6194 (Purple)

This color will be used for:

  • Headings (e.g., h1, h2, h3)
  • Call-to-action buttons (e.g., "Book Now", "Submit")
  • Hero section background
Secondary Color: #6a378b (Dark Purple)

This color will be used for:

  • Hover effects on buttons and links
  • Accent elements such as borders and icons
Background Color: #f4f4f4 (Light Gray)

This color will be used for the background of the body and sections to ensure content readability.

5. Typography

Font 1: Roboto, sans-serif

Usage: This font will be used for the body text to maintain clarity and readability.

Font 2: Merriweather, serif

Usage: This font will be used for headings (e.g., h1, h2, h3) to give the site a clean, professional, and traditional feel.

6. Wireframe

The wireframe section represents the general layout of the homepage for Sparkling Clean. The wireframe includes the positioning of elements like the navigation menu, hero section, service overview, booking form, testimonials, and footer.

Desktop View:

The desktop layout will be designed to display all sections in a horizontal flow, utilizing larger screen space. Each section will be clearly separated with whitespace to enhance readability.

  • Header: Contains logo and navigation menu.
  • Hero Section: A large, welcoming hero image or background with a call-to-action button for booking services.
  • Services Overview: A brief introduction of the services offered, each with a link to the respective service page.
  • Testimonials: Customer reviews will be displayed to build trust.
  • Contact Form: A form for inquiries or booking, with easy-to-fill-out fields.
  • Footer: Includes contact information, social media links, and terms of service.

Mobile View:

The mobile layout will stack sections vertically to fit smaller screen sizes. Navigation will be collapsible (hamburger menu) for easy access on mobile devices. Form fields will be larger for easy tapping, and images will adjust to fit the screen size.
